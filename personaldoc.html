PERSONAL DOCS

At the end of 2022 I took a class where we would make a different personal doc every week based off a one or two word prompt. These were all made under time crunches due to their deadlines and are all rough/first drafts of ideas and work. With that being said I am very happy and suprised by the work I was able to make during this time. I don't feel like I was able to fully get into and explore each topic to its fullest due to lack of time, but feel like they do open the door a little into these different areas. It's very draining to make one of these but I do really enjoy doing it and may try to make more in the future. Given that I've had much more time to sit with these ideas and think them over since initially making these, I would like to go back into some of them and explore them more, but we'll see.
